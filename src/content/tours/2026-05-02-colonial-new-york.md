A walk through the layers of pre-Manhattan: powwow spots, cobblestone streets, orchards, and Lenape landscapes hiding under the colonial grid. We'll visit Washington Square Park (built atop a cemetery), the city's oldest tree, and trace how colonial rule reshaped Manaháhtaan into Manhattan.
