A walk through Greenwich Village tracing the curve of a buried creek the Lenape called Minetta. We'll read the street grid like a palimpsest — a townhouse offset, a curve in MacDougal, a narrow alley — and connect them to the waterway that's been flowing under our feet since long before there was a Manhattan.
